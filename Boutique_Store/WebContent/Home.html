About Us

We Promise to give our Best!!!

In close proximity to some of Mumbai’s most popular open-air markets, Our Hotel offers comfortable, low-key accommodation with self-catering facilities. Brightly decorated rooms feature tiled floors and Satellite televisions. Ceiling fans and free Wi-Fi are also provided. Our hotel boasts a 24-hour reception desk in addition to a communal kitchen where guests can prepare their own meals. The hotel also offers laundry and room service to all its guests. There are numerous cafes and restaurants close to the hotel premises where guests can sample both International and Indian specialities such as Cafe Primo, Mahesh Lunch Home, Mocambo Cafe and Pratap Lunch Home. Guests staying at the hotel will be within easy reach of many attractions and famous city landmarks including the ISKCON Temple, Mukteshwar Temple, Prithvi Theatre and Phoenix Market City which are all within three miles. R City Mall is just over four miles away..
